\title{Nagata Factoriality}
\author{Arthur Freitas Ramos}
\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
  This entry provides an AFP-style Isabelle/HOL formalization of a
  prime-generated version of Nagata's factoriality theorem.  It develops the
  prime-generated multiplicative-set infrastructure, packages a wrapper layer
  over the AFP localization entry \texttt{Localization\_Ring}, and proves
  record-based descent theorems showing that factoriality descends from a
  localization to the base ring under either a prime-generated or a
  prime-or-unit hypothesis on the multiplicative set.  The current application
  layer also includes an abstract polynomial theorem for localization away the
  polynomial variable \(X\), isolating the Laurent-polynomial route followed in
  the companion Lean development.
\end{abstract}
